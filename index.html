What is SAGG Eco Village?

SAGG Eco Village is a natural farm stay and a regenerative lifestyle retreat located in the Himalayan foothills of Kashmir. Led by ecological entrepreneur Fayaz Ahmad Dar, it's a pioneering endeavour where eco-friendliness meets regenerative business and sustainable community development. By integrating nature, culture, and technology through practices such as natural farming, localised building, and DIY design, SAGG is making sustainability look cool. SAGG Eco Village is the premier destination in Kashmir for regenerative recreation and lifestyle. Come visit and save the planet while having fun doing it!

Our Mission
